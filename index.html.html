About Me

A Nurse with a Passion for Technology

I'm a 21-year-old Registered Nurse from Kogi State, Nigeria — a graduate who believes that healthcare and technology are two of the most powerful forces for positive change in the world.

Growing up in Kogi State, I developed a deep sense of purpose and compassion early on. My education at Sanate Secondary School gave me the discipline and hunger for growth that continues to drive me today. I pursued nursing not just as a career, but as a calling — a commitment to serve, to heal, and to make lives better.

Beyond healthcare, I've discovered a powerful love for technology and web development. I believe the future of healthcare lies at the intersection of compassion and innovation, and I'm actively building the skills to stand at that intersection. When I'm not studying or coding, you'll find me on the football pitch — where teamwork, strategy, and resilience are just as important as they are in any hospital ward.

Beyond healthcare and technology, I am also passionate about cryptocurrency trading and financial markets. As a crypto trader, I continuously study market trends, risk management, and digital assets while exploring opportunities in the evolving world of blockchain and finance. My entrepreneurial mindset drives me to keep learning, adapting, and building new opportunities.
